Beef Stew Recipe

Description

Beef stew is a classic. As the name suggests, it contains meat in a hearty tomato vegetable broth. It is often a Winter dish best served with rice/bread next to a fireplace.

Ingredients

  • Beef (gravy/chuck is the best)
  • Beef stock
  • Tomato paste
  • Vegetables: any you like, but recommend carrots, potato, leek and celery
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cornflower
  • Salt and pepper
  • Red wine
  • Worchester sauce

Steps

  1. Cook beef until just brown
  2. Take out beef and leave in a separate bowl
  3. Fry up onion and garlic
  4. Add celery and cook until slightly brown
  5. Add back beef
  6. Mix cornflower in
  7. Pour the stock in, along with 1 cup red wine and 2 tbsp tomato paste
  8. Add worchester sauce and salt and pepper
  9. Bring to a boil and simmer for 1 hour
  10. Add cut up vegetables and return to a simmer
  11. Simmer for 2 hours
