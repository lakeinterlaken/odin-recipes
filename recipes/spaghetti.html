Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pasta of choice
  • Beef or pork mince
  • Diced tomato
  • Onion and garlic
  • Red wine
  • Salt and pepper
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. Dice and saute onion, garlic and carrots
  2. Add meat and brown
  3. Add diced tomato and red wine
  4. Bring to a boil and simmer
  5. Boil pasta
  6. Serve together
